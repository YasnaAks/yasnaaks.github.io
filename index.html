A lonely glove

“The lost glove is happy.” ― Vladimir Nabokov, Pale Fire


I think that a lost crumpled glove is a perfect metaphor for human loneliness. Though often left unnoticed they can be very expressive and striking in their tragic simplicity. I’ve started my collection as an aesthetic protest against the exclusion of those who are considered to be different, strange and foreign.
